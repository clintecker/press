A provider here is *researched*, not *qualified*: an edition becomes qualified for a provider only when a physically ordered copy passes every inspection point below, scoped to that edition's identity.

## Physical inspection checklist

Every point must pass on a copy ordered through the real route; none may be skipped.

- content
- pagination
- trim
- bleed
- spine
- barcode
- color
- paper
- binding
- packaging
- tracking
