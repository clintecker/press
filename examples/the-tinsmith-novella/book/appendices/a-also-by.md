# Also by Perla Nguyen {.unnumbered}

*The Weight of Salt* (2019). A widowed lighthouse keeper measures out
her grief in the small rituals of a coast that is slowly being sold.

*Low Green Country* (2022). Two estranged sisters spend a wet spring
clearing their mother's flooded farmhouse and the silences inside it.

*What the River Keeps* (2024). A linked collection following one
mill town across a century, told in the objects its people leave behind.
