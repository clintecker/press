# About the author {.unnumbered}

Marisol Vance is a marine naturalist who has spent twenty years walking the
same three miles of the Monterey coast at low tide. She taught intertidal
ecology at the field-station level for a decade before deciding that a good
guide in the hand does more than a lecture, and she wrote this one on the
rocks it describes, checking every animal against the page. She lives within
sight of the water.
